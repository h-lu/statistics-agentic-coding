- 来源: https://www.jmir.org/2025/1/e63550/
- 引用: "Practitioners need to be aware of their limitations when working with AI tools... integrate technical experts in the review process" — Public Finance Journal, 2025

## AI 小专栏 #2: AI 生成相关性分析的陷阱——伪相关与虚假关联

### 搜索词: "spurious correlation AI data analysis 2025 2026"
- 数据点: 北卡州立大学2025年3月发布突破性研究，开发出无需识别虚假特征即可消除虚假相关的数据剪枝方法，发表于ICLR 2025
- 来源: https://news.ncsu.edu/2025/03/ai-spurious-correlations/
- 数据点: "Clever Hans效应"（AI看似智能但依赖误导性线索）的五大成因：数据伪影、缺乏因果监督、数据集不平衡、评估流程不足、可解释性工具缺乏
- 来源: https://www.frontiersin.org/journals/artificial-intelligence/articles/10.3389/frai.2025.1692454/full
- 数据点: KDD 2025研究显示，使用证据深度学习（Evidential Deep Learning）可以自动加权高不确定性样本，提升对虚假相关的鲁棒性
- 来源: https://blog.csdn.net/c_cpp_csharp/article/details/156187951

### 搜索词: "AI hallucination statistics false correlations"
- 数据点: Statsig 2025年分析指出，相关性分析常见陷阱包括：相关≠因果、虚假相关、多重共线性掩盖真实驱动因素、隐藏变量造成误导性关联
- 来源: https://www.statsig.com/perspectives/ai-eval-correlation-techniques
- 引用: "Causal AI does not merely look at correlations, but it rather allows study of the causal relationships that are embedded in the data" — Risk Management Magazine, 2025
- 来源: https://www.aifirm.it/wp-content/uploads/2025/04/RMM-2025-01-Excerpt-1.pdf
