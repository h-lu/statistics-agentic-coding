## AI 小专栏 #1: 分类评估陷阱——准确率不够用了

### 搜索词: "accuracy paradox imbalanced classification 2026"
- 数据点: 准确率悖论（Accuracy Paradox）——在类别不平衡场景中，总是预测多数类可以获得 99%+ 的准确率，但完全漏掉少数类
- 来源: [Wikipedia - Accuracy Paradox](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Accuracy_paradox)
- 引用: "The accuracy paradox is the paradoxical finding that accuracy is not a good metric for predictive models when classifying in predictive analytics."
- 数据点: 2025 年有系统性综述研究不平衡学习问题，涵盖 SMOTE 悖论框架和部署差距
- 来源: [A Systematic Review of 821 Papers in Imbalanced Learning (2020-2026)](https://openreview.net/pdf/9c2578fa8ada491ed679a6f3115b96ec6d2ecab0.pdf)
- 数据点: 2025 年 9 月有研究专门讨论"使用增强加权性能指标解决准确率悖论"
- 来源: [ResearchGate - Addressing Accuracy Paradox](https://www.researchgate.net/publication/340894484_Addressing_Accuracy_Paradox_Using_Enhanched_Weighted_Performance_Metric_in_Machine_Learning)
- 数据点: 不平衡分类场景下，F1 分数和 PR-AUC 比准确率更可靠的评估指标
- 来源: [Machine Learning Mastery - Failure of Accuracy](https://www.machinelearningmastery.com/failure-of-accuracy-for-imbalanced-class-distributions/)
- 数据点: 类别不平衡会导致模型偏向多数类，影响欺诈检测、医疗诊断等高风险领域
- 来源: [Towards AI - Imbalanced Classification](https://pub.towardsai.net/imbalanced-classification-d58a1eaa3cf7)

### 搜索词: "F1 score AUC imbalanced data business 2026"
- 数据点: 2025 年 12 月的实证研究比较了准确率、精确率、召回率、F1 和 MCC 在商业预测模型中的表现
- 来源: [Journal of Big Data - Springer 2025](https://link.springer.com/article/10.1186/s40537-025-01313-4)
- 引用: "Imbalanced datasets pose a persistent challenge in business data mining, particularly in high-stakes domains such as financial risk prediction and customer churn analysis"
- 数据点: PR-AUC（精确率-召回率曲线下面积）在不平衡数据上比 ROC-AUC 更现实
- 来源: [Medium - ML Evaluation Metrics](https://premvishnoi.medium.com/machine-learning-evaluation-metrics-accuracy-f1-roc-auc-explained-simply-6d27039124de)
- 引用: "PR-AUC gives a more realistic picture than ROC-AUC for imbalanced data by focusing on the minority class"
- 数据点: iCert Global 社区讨论了不平衡数据集的最可靠评估指标
- 来源: [iCert Global - Best Evaluation Metrics](https://www.icertglobal.com/community/best-evaluation-metrics-for-imbalanced-predictive-models-)
- 引用: "I always use AUPRC (Area Under Precision-Recall Curve). It's far more sensitive to performance of positive class than standard ROC curve."

## AI 小专栏 #2: 数据泄漏——AI 时代最常见的统计错误

### 搜索词: "data leakage cross-validation pipeline 2026"
- 数据点: 在交叉验证之前对整个数据集做预处理（如 StandardScaler）会导致数据泄漏
- 来源: [Medium - Prevent Data Leakage in Cross-Validation 2025](https://medium.com/@pacosun/split-smart-avoiding-data-leakage-in-cross-validation-894863a93553)
- 引用: "The sneaky one. When you scale your entire dataset before splitting, you're telling your model about your test set's distribution"
- 数据点: scikit-learn 官方文档明确指出预处理不一致会导致模型性能评估偏差
- 来源: [scikit-learn - Common Pitfalls](https://scikit-learn.org/stable/common_pitfalls.html)
- 引用: "If these data transforms are used when training a model, they also must be used on subsequent datasets, whether it's test data"
- 数据点: 使用 Pipeline 确保预处理在每个折内独立执行，是防止数据泄漏的标准做法
- 来源: [Towards Data Science - Pipeline to Prevent Leakage](https://towardsdatascience.com/pre-process-data-with-pipeline-to-prevent-data-leakage-during-cross-validation-e3442cca7fdc)
- 引用: "For 5-fold cross-validation, data ratio of inner train set to validation set may not be preserved"
- 数据点: 训练集和测试集之间的数据泄漏会导致虚高的评估指标
- 来源: [Towards Data Science - How to prevent Data Leakage](https://towardsdatascience.com/how-to-avoid-data-leakage-while-evaluating-the-performance-of-a-machine-learning-model-ac30f2bb8586/)
- 引用: "Data leakage occurs when data from training set passes into your validation/test set"
